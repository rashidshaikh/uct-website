A Smarter World

Internet of Things (IOT) is about creating situation awareness, responding to environment stimulus and making inferences from the data acquired.

Transformation

Internet of Things is going to transform the way we live our lives.

Technology

IOT involves data acquisition from devices, transporting that data to a high computation and large storage infrastructure using various communication protocols

Extension of Web

With the advent of seamless sensing at mega scale, analysis on big data, information representation on multiple form factors and service oriented integration, IOT has come to its natural extension of web

Our Services

We provide efficient and cost effective IoT solutions for various businesses using ubiquitous connectivity and interactive technologies. Our mission is to be a recognized and successful IOT services provider

 IOT Consulting & Implementation

We offer consulting services by comprehending the Business processes and evaluating the current technology landscape. Whether its upgrading the connected services to the new IOT platforms or enabling business reach IOT capability from the scratch, we provide a holistic and optimal adoption of Internet of Things products and solution as per our customer's needs

 IOT Platform Evaluation & Development

For all the industry verticals that we work on, their IOT use cases and requirements vary quite a lot. And our solution offerings have to be in line with the industry requirements. Similary the IOT platforms that we pick e.g. AWS or Microsoft Azure IOT, Axeda etc. are based on our evaluation on as per case basis

 Sensor & Gateway Selection

Central to the concept of IOT is Device Connectivity. Devices and sensors are either smart themselves in which case they can send data to an IOT platform or we need to provide gateway like Intel, Arduino, Digi Gateway which takes sensor data on one side and send it to cloud platform on the other side. Our services include consulting and development on Sensors and Gateways too
